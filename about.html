About Me

Hi there. My name is Natalie Bloomston, some call me Spoon. Once upon a time, I was a graduate student at the University of Wyoming. Now, I'm taking a different approach to learning, as I was never really cut out for the traditional education setting.

Alabama is home and where my roots belong. While I explore new ventures and project ideas, I'll be writing about what I learn, sharing various tidbits I call "spoons," and offering music recommendations whenever relevant (so always).

I'm working on revamping this website from the course assignment it started as, but I hope someone finds something useful in whatever I find to share along the way.
